# AI Developer Learning Paths — 5-Level Roadmap for 2026

> **YouTube / Industry Analysis** · Educational Brief · `ai_coding` · 2026-04-02

---

## TL;DR

AI developer skills in 2026 have shifted from ML theory to practical integration and orchestration. The modern progression is Cook (use AI) → Chef (build with AI) → Orchestrator (deploy AI systems). Core competencies: prompt engineering, RAG/MCP, multimodal integration, agent frameworks, and "vibe coding." The money is in integration, not algorithms.

## Key Insights

- **Five-level progression.** (1) Foundations & PRD method, (2) Context/RAG/MCP, (3) Multimodal one-person agency, (4) AI agents with orchestration, (5) Vibe coding — high-level intent → working code.
- **Eight critical competencies (Tina Huang).** Gen AI literacy, prompt engineering, specialized tools, AI agents, open-source AI, AI-assisted coding, future trends awareness, continuous learning.
- **Three income lanes.** Employee ($200K+ at OpenAI/Meta/Anthropic), Consultant (business AI integration), Builder (AI-powered SaaS products and tools).
- **Market not saturated despite hype.** High churn (most quit 30-60 days), no institutional players in tooling yet. Opportunity for serious operators who stick with it.
- **MCP is the new integration layer.** Model Context Protocol for connecting AI to external data/tools is emerging as the standard — already deployed in Andy's agent infrastructure.
