# LIFT Summit 2026 — Full Program & Companies

> **Monterey Bay DART** · March 31–April 2 · `uas` · 2026-03-22

## TL;DR

3-day AAM/UAS summit at Monterey Conference Center. **Day 1 (March 31) is Expo Day** — live flight demos at AirSpace Integration test range + La Selva Beach, airfield tours, VIP reception at Monterey Jet Center. Days 2-3 are conference sessions on FAA Part 108, eVTOL infrastructure, workforce, airspace modernization. AirSpace Integration is a venue host for the flight demos.

## Schedule

### March 31 — Expo Day (THIS IS YOUR DAY)
- **8am–3pm**: Airfield tours + live flight demonstrations
  - Location: **AirSpace Integration UAS Test Range, La Selva Beach** + Marina Municipal Airport
- **5pm–8pm**: VIP Opening Reception at Monterey Jet Center

### April 1 — Conference Day 1
- FAA eIPP rollout + AAM National Strategy fireside chat
- Opening Plenary: federal/state aviation authorities (FAA, USDOT, NASAO)
- State eIPP proposals: New York, Ohio, Texas, California
- Part 108 investor panel (Diamond Stream + AAM CEOs)
- Community acceptance panel
- DART workforce development showcase
- Workforce pipeline panel (manufacturing, maintenance, UAS ops)

### April 2 — Conference Day 2
- Airport/vertiport financing (P3 models, capital stack)
- Vertiport energy/charging infrastructure (PG&E, utilities, microgrids)
- Public service UAS: emergency response, healthcare, disaster (NASA, FAA)
- Digital airspace infrastructure (UTM, data-sharing, cyber-resilience)
- Weather integration for low-altitude flight
- eVTOL airport integration lessons
- Closing: 12-24 month action commitments
