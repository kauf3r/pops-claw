## TL;DR

Simple but powerful maintenance habit: periodically review MEMORY.md and migrate reusable patterns to skills or TOOLS.md. Memory should be curated institutional knowledge, not a dumping ground for operational instructions.

## Key Insights

- MEMORY.md should be curated context, not how-to instructions
- Reusable workflows belong in skills (SKILL.md files)
- Environment-specific config belongs in TOOLS.md
- Regular pruning prevents memory bloat and keeps retrieval quality high
- Bob's MEMORY.md is currently 80+ lines — good candidate for audit
