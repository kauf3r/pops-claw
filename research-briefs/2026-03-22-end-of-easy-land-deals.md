## TL;DR

JB (started 2016, 6-7 figure years 2019-2022, ~$5M current inventory) explains why blind mail campaigns and small-lot double closes are dying. The future is expertise-based: large subdivision deals, problem properties with title issues, and on-market FSBO opportunities where selling intent is already established. Volume is out; margin is in.

## Key Insights

- Blind offers at full asking price are now returning zero deals -- the problem is oversaturation, not pricing
- Direct mail ROI has collapsed; shift to on-market properties and FSBOs where selling intent already exists
- "Paper rehabilitation" (fixing liens, heirship, quiet title) is the new moat -- most investors avoid complexity
- Scale vs. margin trade-off: large subdivision deals ($300K-$5M) beat high-volume small flips
- 3 of 5 top investors in REtipster Ep. 250 confirmed they're moving to subdivisions

## The One Thing

**Shift from volume-based to expertise-based positioning in land investing.**

The easy arbitrage is gone. The defensible advantage now is being the person who can solve complex title issues and write large checks -- the "rich uncle" that others bring deals to because the problems are too hard for generalists.
